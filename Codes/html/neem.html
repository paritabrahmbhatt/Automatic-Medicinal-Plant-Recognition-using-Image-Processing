Azadirachta indica / Margosa Tree / Neem Tree



Neem is a fast-growing evergreen tree that is highly valued for its medicinal properties. The scientific name of the neem tree is Azadirachta indica. It is also referred to as nim or margosa. The neem tree is native to India and other dry areas in South Asia. It was later introduced in Africa and is now grown in many places in both continents. It was also added to the Caribbean, as well as South and Central America. The tree’s timber and leaves have been used in Ayurvedic medicines for centuries. They are now also used in makeup and organic farming.
